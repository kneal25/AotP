More Info on the Black Lives Matter Movement Action of the People

The past few years have been especially tumultuous for activists, allies, and minorities alike.
The Black Lives Matter movement, or "BLM", is a movement that holds various events
such as marches, rallies, protests, fundraisers, and more as to recieve justice for
for the oppression that minorities, mainly the Black community, has faced.
One large oppressor of this group are actually the police, who are meant to be
the protectors of America. There have been countless murders where police
kill Black people brutally, and without hesitating. One phrase that observes the
corrupt police is the phrase, "ACAB," standing for "All cops are bastards."
While this phrase does seem aggressive or even hurtful, all it's truly saying
is that all cops, whether "good," or "bad," work for a corrupt system.

It is so important now more than ever to educate ourselves on racism and other prominent topics.
This way, we can work to the goal of ending discrimination in America.

Things you can do to educate yourself:

  • Access websites that are trustworthy and informative

  • Ask friends and family

  • Attend events where you can learn more about current topics
